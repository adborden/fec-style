4.6 #components.cards Cards

Small boxes that contain content Default layout is image stacked on text. All sizing is handled by using .grid and .grid__item, either as containers or on the cards themselves

   <img alt="" src="../img/i-calendar--primary.svg" class="icon--complex card__image">
   <div class="card__content">
     <a href="#">Take this action</a>
   </div>
 </aside>
</div>
